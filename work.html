Case Studies

Nexxen DSP — Design System Rebuild 2026

Rebuilt a legacy design system from primitives up for a programmatic advertising platform. The existing system had grown without structure: hard-coded values scattered across surfaces, no token architecture, and no path to consistent retheming. I established a 3-tier token structure in Figma, rebuilt 200+ components, consolidated 40+ hard-coded values, and delivered dark mode and white-label support that the team had been blocked on for two product cycles.

This was not a visual refresh. It was a systems infrastructure project that changed how the whole team shipped.

ATLAS — Special Education Case Management 2025

New York's SESIS platform was retiring after two decades. ATLAS was its replacement: a statewide IEP management system serving 250,000 students across 1,800+ schools, with federal compliance timelines that could not slip.

I owned the evaluator assignment module end-to-end, from discovery interviews with case managers and school psychologists to final component specs. The result was a 40% reduction in tracking time and a workflow that evaluators could use without a training manual.
